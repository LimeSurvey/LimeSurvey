For package size reasons the migmix-1p-regular.ttf font to show Japanese characters in the statistics graphs 
is not included with LimeSurvey.

Please download it from http://mix-mplus-ipa.sourceforge.jp/migmix/ , 
unzip and put the migmix-1p-regular.ttf file into the /assets/fonts directory of your GititSurvey installation.
